Frequently asked questions

  • How long does it take to deliver cakes?

    • Normal cakes order can be made one day prior. For premium and Customised cakes, order should be made 3 days prior. Additional charges are applicable for customised cakes.

  • How long does it take to deliver pastries?

    • Normally baking takes 40 mins and frosting takes 2-3 hours. Thus, you can expect delivery in 5 hours

  • Is there any delivery charges?

    • No delivery fees within 5 km radius. Delivery charges will added based on delivery location.
